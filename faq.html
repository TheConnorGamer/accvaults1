Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AccVaults

General Questions

What is AccVaults?

AccVaults is a premium digital services marketplace offering affordable access to streaming services, gaming accounts, software licenses, and more. We provide instant delivery and 24/7 customer support.

How do I make a purchase?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept various payment methods through our secure payment processor, Paylix. After payment, you'll receive your product details instantly via email.

Is it safe to buy from AccVaults?

Yes! All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways with encryption. We never store your payment information. Our products are legitimate and sourced responsibly.

Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Most products are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. You'll receive an email with your product details within minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Visit our Downloads page and enter your order email to view all your purchases and their status. You can also check your order confirmation email for direct links.

What if my product doesn't work?

We offer automatic replacement for any non-working products. Contact our support team via Discord or email with your order details, and we'll provide a replacement within 24 hours.

Payment & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, cryptocurrency, and various other payment methods through our secure payment processor. All major payment options are available at checkout.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally don't offer refunds. However, if a product is defective or not as described, we'll provide a replacement or issue a refund on a case-by-case basis. Please review our Terms of Service for full details.

Are there any hidden fees?

No! The price you see is the price you pay. There are no hidden fees or charges. Payment processing fees (if any) are clearly displayed at checkout.

Account & Support

Do I need an account to make a purchase?

No, you can checkout as a guest using just your email address. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, view purchase history, and receive exclusive deals.

How can I contact support?

Join our Discord server for the fastest support response, or use our Contact Us page to send us a message. We typically respond within 1-2 hours during business hours.

Do you have a warranty or guarantee?

Yes! All products come with our quality guarantee. If any product stops working within the warranty period (varies by product), we'll provide a free replacement. Check individual product descriptions for specific warranty details.
